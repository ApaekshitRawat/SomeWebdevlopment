Gymnasium (Where Gods train!!)

A gymnasium, also known as a gym, is a covered location for athletics.
The word is derived from the ancient Greek gymnasium.[1] They are commonly
found in athletic and fitness centers, and as activity and learning spaces
in educational institutions. "Gym" is also slang for "fitness center", which
is often an area for indoor recreation. A gym may be open air as well. A gym
is a place with number of equipments and machines used by the people to do exercises.
